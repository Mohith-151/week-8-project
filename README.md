# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy
**Capstone Project: Real-World Business Analysis**

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end analysis of customer attrition to identify why users are leaving and how the business can intervene. By analyzing patterns in the `customer_churn.csv` dataset, we aim to provide data-driven recommendations to improve long-term customer retention.

## ❗ Business Problem Statement
The company is experiencing customer churn that directly impacts monthly recurring revenue. We need to identify the key drivers of this attrition—whether they are related to pricing, service usage, or contract types—and provide an actionable implementation plan to the marketing and customer success teams.

## 🚀 Objectives
*   **Identify Drivers:** Determine the top 3 factors leading to customer churn.
*   **Risk Profiling:** Create a "High-Risk" customer segment profile for targeted interventions.
*   **Actionable Insights:** Recommend at least three specific business strategy changes based on data patterns.

## 📈 Success Metrics
*   **Model Accuracy:** Identification of churn patterns with clear statistical significance.
*   **Actionability:** Production of at least 5 professional visualizations that "tell the story" to non-technical stakeholders.
*   **Implementation Plan:** A step-by-step roadmap for reducing churn based on findings.

## 📂 Project Structure
*   `data/`: Contains `raw_data.csv` and `cleaned_data.csv`.
*   `notebooks/`: Modular Jupyter Notebooks for Cleaning, EDA, and Advanced Analysis.
*   `reports/`: Executive summary (PDF) and technical documentation.
*   `presentation/`: Final business slide deck.
*   `requirements.txt`: List of Python libraries required to run the analysis.
